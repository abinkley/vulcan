Grasshopper Kids Race

Welcome to the Grasshopper Kids Race!

We're excited to give you all the information you will need for our upcoming events. The Grasshopper Kids Race is designed to introduce children to the joy of cycling in a safe, supportive environment.

Event Information

Schedule: Saturday September 6, 2025

Location: Kid's Korse Along Rail Trail below Cedar Grove Community Center, Fairview Avenue Cedar Grove, NJ

Questions? alex.binkley@gmail.com

What to Expect

Our Grasshopper Kids Race is a fun competitive race for young kids!

Age Groups

  • Grades K-1 0.6 miles (no balance bikes)
  • Grades 2-3: 0.9 miles
  • Grades 4-5: 1.2 miles
