Vulcan Cycling: Dominant Weekend at Tree House Cross & Cooper River!

The Vulcan squad had an incredible weekend of racing, taking home multiple wins and podiums across two days of cyclocross action in New Jersey!

Tree House Cross – Day 1 (October 18, 2025)

The weekend kicked off strong at Tree House Cross, where Tamara Warner powered to victory in the Cat 4 Women race, while Niko Warner continued his dominant season, taking 1st place in Junior Men 11–12.

A perfect start to the weekend for Team Vulcan — two riders, two wins!

Cooper River CX – (October 19, 2025)

Sunday’s racing brought even more excitement and hardware for Vulcan riders:

Tamara Warner – 4th place, Cat 3/4/5 Women

Nora Phillips – 1st place, Junior Women 9–10

Juno Miller – 6th place, Junior Men 11–12

Gunnar Miller – 27th place, Cat 4 Men

Niko Warner – 1st place, Junior Men 11–12

Xenia Binkley – 1st place, Junior Women 11–12

Vulcan riders brought home three wins across six categories, showing the team’s strength across all age groups and divisions. Special shoutout to our youngest riders, who swept both junior women’s categories!

Weekend Highlights

🏆 Five 1st-place finishes (Tamara, Nora, Niko, and Xenia)

💪 Strong performances across multiple categories

🌟 Continued growth from our youth riders, setting the tone for the rest of the season

Congrats to all riders and families for another successful weekend!
